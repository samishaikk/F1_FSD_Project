Teams & Drivers

Discover the incredible teams and talented drivers competing in Formula 1.

Top Teams in Formula 1

Formula 1 features world-class teams that push the limits of engineering and technology to win championships.

Mercedes AMG Petronas

Known for their dominance in the hybrid era, Mercedes has been the team to beat since 2014.

Scuderia Ferrari

The most iconic and successful team in F1 history, Ferrari has been competing since 1950.

Red Bull Racing

Renowned for their innovative designs, Red Bull is the team behind drivers like Max Verstappen.

Alpine F1 Team

Alpine is Renault's rebranded F1 team, blending performance and innovation.

McLaren

One of the oldest teams in F1, McLaren is famous for its legacy of champions.

Aston Martin

Returning to F1, Aston Martin brings elegance and high-performance engineering.

Legendary Drivers

Meet the talented drivers who compete at the pinnacle of motorsport.

Lewis Hamilton

With 7 world championships, Lewis is one of the most successful drivers in F1 history.

Max Verstappen

A two-time world champion, Verstappen is known for his aggressive driving and skill.

Charles Leclerc

A rising star in Formula 1, Leclerc represents the future of Scuderia Ferrari.

Fernando Alonso

A two-time world champion, Alonso is one of F1's most experienced and skillful drivers.

Sebastian Vettel

With 4 world titles, Vettel is a legend in F1 and an advocate for sustainability.

Lando Norris

A young talent from McLaren, Lando is admired for his pace and personality.
