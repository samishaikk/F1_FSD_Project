Tracks & Races

Discover the legendary circuits and unforgettable moments that define Formula 1's history.

Iconic Tracks

Monaco Grand Prix

The Monaco Grand Prix is one of the most prestigious and challenging circuits in Formula 1. Nestled in the streets of Monte Carlo, it tests drivers with its narrow roads, sharp turns, and unforgiving barriers. The glamorous surroundings make it a fan-favorite.

Silverstone Circuit

Known as the home of British motorsport, Silverstone hosted the first-ever Formula 1 race in 1950. With its high-speed corners and rich history, it remains one of the most iconic tracks in the F1 calendar.

Suzuka Circuit

The Suzuka Circuit in Japan is unique for its figure-eight layout. It has been the stage for many championship-deciding races and is beloved for its challenging design.

Spa-Francorchamps

Located in Belgium, Spa-Francorchamps is renowned for its long straights and the famous Eau Rouge corner. It is a favorite among drivers and fans alike for its picturesque setting and thrilling layout.

Schumacher's First Win (1992 Spa)

Michael Schumacher claimed his first-ever Formula 1 victory at Spa in 1992. It was the beginning of an extraordinary career that would see him become one of the sport's greatest legends.

2019 Austrian GP

The 2019 Austrian Grand Prix was a race for the ages, with Max Verstappen coming back from seventh place to win in a dramatic final few laps.

2021 Abu Dhabi Finale

The 2021 season finale in Abu Dhabi was one of the most controversial and dramatic races in F1 history, with Max Verstappen securing his first championship in a nail-biting last-lap battle.
